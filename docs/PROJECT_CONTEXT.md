Version: 0.1

Last Updated: 27 June 2026

---

# Vision

Mr. AI Investor is an AI-powered investment research assistant that helps people understand businesses before investing.

The goal is **not** to tell users what stock to buy.

The goal is to teach users how to think like investors by providing structured, explainable, and evidence-based analysis.

The product should feel like talking to an experienced portfolio manager who explains every decision clearly.

---

# Mission Statement

> "Help me understand this business so I can decide for myself."

---

# Problem Statement

Most investing tools assume users already understand finance.

Beginners often struggle with:

* Understanding what a company actually does
* Knowing whether financial statements are healthy
* Understanding competitors
* Understanding industry trends
* Knowing what risks matter
* Deciding whether a company fits their investment goals

Professionals have the opposite problem.

They spend hours collecting information from multiple sources before making decisions.

Mr. AI Investor combines these scattered tasks into one intelligent workflow.

---

# Product Philosophy

This product should never blindly recommend buying or selling.

Instead it should explain:

* Why
* How
* What evidence supports the conclusion
* What assumptions are being made

The user always makes the final decision.
